FAQ

What if books had a lovely comments section?

LoveBooks is the place you can talk about your favorite books, and see what other people are saying. We only have one rule: let's keep it positive. This is not a reviews website. You're not here to write an essay.

This is the space where you can share with people all those little details that got stuck into your mind about the books you've read, and find people that love the same stories as you!

Let's start.

Sign up for an account here. If you already have one, simply log in to be able to make comments.

Go to our home page. There you can type on the Search Box the name of a book or author. If you don't find what you're looking for, registered users can add new books.

After logging in, users will find on the menu the option to ADD NEW BOOKS. Then you'll just new to wait for the approval and everyone will be able to talk about your new addition to our catalogue!
